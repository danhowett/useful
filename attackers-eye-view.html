Attacker's Eye View · Interactive narrative

See your defence through attacker eyes.

Pick a scenario. Play the attacker for a series of decisions across the kill chain. At each stage, you see what the attacker observes, what the defender sees, and the options the attacker would consider. Pick one; see what would happen and which control would (or wouldn't) catch it. End with a defender debrief showing the controls that mattered, the blind spots exposed, and the real-world incident this most resembles.

Five hand-built scenarios, each 5-7 stages, ~10 minutes per run. No live offensive techniques — all decisions are at the category level. The defender's perspective is the point.

!
Orientation, not threat modelling. This is a thinking aid built to reveal defender blind spots through attacker perspective — not a substitute for organisation-specific threat assessment, red teaming, or intelligence analysis. Scenarios are calibrated to attacker class and defender archetype, not to your specific environment.
