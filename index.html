Interactive Narrative · Learning
v1.0 New

Attacker's Eye View

See your defence through attacker eyes. Five hand-built narrative scenarios spanning financial crime, nation-state espionage, ransomware-as-a-service, OT disruption, and mass exploitation. Play the attacker through 5-7 stages per scenario, choosing moves at each step — recon, initial access, foothold, discovery, privilege escalation, action on objectives. Each choice reveals the defender control that catches it (or doesn't), and the run ends with a defender debrief showing the controls that mattered, the blind spots exposed, and the real-world incident it most resembles. No live offensive techniques — all decisions at the category level.

OT Incident · Triage
v1.0 New

OT Incident Triage

Safety-first decision support for IT-trained responders facing an OT alert. Triage starts with the asset (EWS, HMI, historian, PLC/RTU, SIS, OT network, vendor jump host, or unknown) and layers criticality on top. Five safety gates determine whether the decision is Proceed-Limited, Hold-for-OT, or Full-Stop. Surfaces the IT-instinct trap for each asset class (isolate / image / reset) and the safer path, plus explicit allowed and prohibited actions, non-invasive evidence collection, and the call list before any action. Aligned with NIST SP 800-82r3, IEC 62443 and NCSC OT guidance.
