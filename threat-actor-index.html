Who are these people?

A searchable reference index of 68 threat actor groups — nation-state, financially motivated, hacktivist, and mixed-motivation. Each entry gives a three-sentence summary covering who they are, what they're known for, and current status. Search by name or alias (the same group is often known by five or more names across vendors), or filter by motivation, attribution, and operational status. Sits alongside the Adversary Mindsets tool (which works at the disposition layer) and the ATT&CK Mapper (technique layer).
