Stage 02 / Assess

Score programme maturity across five dimensions

For each control, pick the tier that best matches your current state. Tag evidence status and capture a short note — this powers the exports and improvement plan. There's no penalty for honest L1s; an accurate picture beats an aspirational one.

Stage 04 / Plan

Improvement actions

Auto-generated from L1/L2 controls and flagged anti-patterns, weighted by impact-vs-effort. Assign owners and due dates — these will export with your assessment for the next review cycle.

Tip: Address red anti-pattern flags first regardless of tier. A programme with high simulation maturity but a punitive response culture is worse than no programme at all.
Stage 05 / Export

Take it with you

Three output formats. JSON for ingestion by CIRA / ICCC. PDF for the board pack. Printable snapshot for the wall, the team briefing, or the slide deck.

⎔

JSON

Schema: dh-phishing-maturity v1. Compatible with CIRA and ICCC import. Includes all controls, evidence status, scores, flags, and action plan.

▤

Printable snapshot

One-page summary — overall score, radar, heatmap and top flags. A4 portrait. Suitable for the team briefing wall or appendix to a board pack.

◫

Board PDF

Full narrative report — programme summary, dimension breakdowns, anti-pattern findings, improvement plan with owners and due dates. Print to PDF from your browser.
