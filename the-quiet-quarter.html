The Quiet Quarter

A patient adversary. A narrow window. Eight decisions that don't have right answers.

You are the Incident Commander. At 03:14 last night your SOC flagged a single suspicious sign-in. By Friday evening you'll know what kind of organisation yours is — not because the threat will tell you, but because the choices you make in the next eight turns will.

Every choice spends from a budget you can see. Every choice also sets consequences you can't — yet. There are multiple paths to a good outcome, and more paths to bad ones. At the end, you'll see what was actually happening behind the alerts.

Pre-game briefing

Your organisation is mid-sized (~1,500 staff), UK-based, regulated under UK GDPR. You hold significant customer PII and some sensitive HR records. You have a competent SOC, a Veeam backup estate, an EDR rollout that's ~85% complete, a managed service provider for infrastructure, and a cyber insurance policy with a £25M limit. Today is Tuesday morning.

Tip: use 1 2 3 4 to pick choices, Space to continue.
