The four maturity stages

Initial
Ad-hoc, manual, undocumented. Reactive and inconsistent — gaps are common and unmonitored.
Developing
Documented and partially implemented, but inconsistent coverage and limited automation or enforcement.
Defined
Standardised, broadly enforced and largely automated across the estate, with measurement in place.
Optimised
Continuously verified, risk-adaptive and self-correcting. Fully automated with active threat detection.
