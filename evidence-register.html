Incident Response · DFIR · Evidence Register

Account for every item.

A lightweight register of evidence collected during an incident — physical devices and digital artefacts alike. Capture what it is, when and where it was seized, who took it, and where it lives now. Tag each item to a host or IOC to tie it back to your timeline. This is an inventory for findability and accounting — not a chain-of-custody instrument. Everything stays in your browser.
