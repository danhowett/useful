Think before you contain.

A decision-support tool for the IC under pressure. Pick your architecture profile and the threat scenario you're facing, answer 3-5 narrowing questions, and the tool surfaces a ranked, annotated set of containment options — with seven trade-off dimensions, profile-specific warnings, and rationale capture for the post-incident review. Not a runbook: this is a decision aid for thinking through what containment buys you and costs you.
